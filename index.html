Hajime Isayama is a Japanese manga artist, best known for creating the globally acclaimed series Attack on Titan. He was born on August 29, 1986, in Ōyama, Ōita Prefecture, Japan. Isayama’s distinctive storytelling style and dark themes have earned him international recognition and numerous awards.

While working on Attack on Titan, Isayama developed a complex world filled with moral ambiguity and intense human conflict. His dedication and attention to detail made the series a modern manga masterpiece.
